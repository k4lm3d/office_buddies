# 🕵️‍♂️ MISSION: OFFICE BUDDIES
**A Custom Community Case for PeekAPhone**

## 🛑 ACCESS AUTHORIZATION
* **Status:** Operational
* **Target Device:** Lovely Loren's Mobile Phone
* **Time Window:** Critical (Subject is currently in the warehouse)
* **Classification:** Fan-Made Community Intel

## 📁 CASE BRIEFING
Lovely Loren is keeping a massive secret from her supervisor, Katie Leslie. Rumor has it that Loren is planning to resign from the company to return to university. Because the two are incredibly close, Loren is terrified of hurting Katie's feelings and disrupting the team. 

However, finding competent staff is exceptionally difficult for Katie. She desperately needs to know Loren's final decision so she can properly prepare for a potential departure and begin recruiting. 

Loren has just stepped away to the warehouse. This is your only window of opportunity. Help Katie bypass the security on Loren's phone and discover the truth before she returns.

## 🎯 OBJECTIVES
1. **Gain Access:** Intercept and bypass the security locks on Lovely Loren's personal device.
2. **Snoop the Data:** Scour her chat logs, emails, and apps for clues regarding her academic plans.
3. **Uncover the Truth:** Determine once and for all if she is leaving the company or staying.
4. **Be Fast:** Complete the extraction before Loren returns from the warehouse.

## 👥 INTEL PROFILES

* **Katie Leslie (The Supervisor):** Loyal, stressed, and trying to keep her department afloat. She values her bond with Loren but needs to protect the company.
* **Lovely Loren (The Subject):** A dedicated employee who is torn between her personal ambitions and her loyalty to her close friend/boss.
